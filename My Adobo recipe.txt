Chicken and Pork Adobo

1 tbsp oil
4 cloves garlic
1 kl chicken
1 kl pork
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup vinegar
1 cup water
2 tbsp oyster sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar
bay leaves
black pepper

1. Saute onion until tender.
2. Add the pork and saute until color has changed.
3. Add the chicken and saute until color has changed.
4. Add soy sauce, vinegar and water
